# Interflect Philosophy

Interflect exists to make Reflect → Compound real across fragmented agent
sessions.

Reflect without Compound is journaling: the system notices lessons but future
sessions do not change. Compound without Reflect is cargo-culting: durable rules
change without evidence of why. Interflect holds the seam by preserving source
handles, classifying lessons, and asking for review before durable promotion.

## Principles

1. **Source before synthesis.** Every proposal needs a session/source handle and
   snippet.
2. **Proposal before mutation.** v0 emits reviewable proposals, not silent canon
   rewrites.
3. **Right substrate, smallest durable update.** Memory, skills, repo doctrine,
   Beads, and routing overlays are different targets with different approval
   boundaries.
4. **Runtime state is not canon.** Transient facts should decay unless a review
   shows durable value.
5. **Boundaries preserve signal.** Interflect internalizes lessons; Interspect
   profiles performance.
